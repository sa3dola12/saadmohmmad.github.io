Our Recipes' Preparation Methods

Omlet

  1. Beat the eggs
  2. Heat the pan
  3. Cook the eggs
  4. Add fillings
  5. Fold and serve

Ful medames

  1. Heat the beans
  2. Mash the beans with a fork
  3. Add garlic, cumin, salt and lemon
  4. Drizzle olive oil on top
  5. Serve with tomatoes

Cheesy Fries

  1. Place the boiled potatoes in a pan
  2. Fry them with oil until become crispy
  3. Sprinkle cheese sauce and herbs on top
  4. Cover the pan for a minute and serve it

Manakish

  1. Mix the dough ingredients and let it rise for an hour
  2. Cut the dough into pieces
  3. Spread the zaatar,cheese and oil on top
  4. place in a very hot preheated oven at 200°C for 10 minutes until become golden and crispy

Negresco

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Cook the chicken with the onion,garlic and mushrooms
  3. In a pot melt the butter then add flour and stir to make the sauce
  4. Mix the pasta with part of the sauce
  5. Transfer everything into a baking dish, pour the remaining sauce on top and sprinkle with mozzarella
  6. Bake until the top is golden and the cheese is melted

Hawawshi

  1. Mix the minced meat with onion,pepper and spices
  2. Stuff the mixture inside bread
  3. Brush the bread with oil
  4. Cook in a pan with medium heat, turn until cooked
  5. Serve hot with pickles

Maqlooba

  1. Fry eggplant and vegetables until golden
  2. Cook chicken with onion, garlic and spices, then add water to make broth
  3. In a pot , put the vegetables first, then the rice, then the chicken
  4. Pour broth over until rice is covered
  5. Cook on low heat until rice is done
  6. Flip the pot onto a serving plate and serve hot

Shawarma

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, lemon, vinegar and spices
  2. Cook in a pan on medium heat until fully done
  3. Warm the bread
  4. Fill with shawarma and add garlic sauce
  5. Serve with fries or pickles

Brownies

  1. Melt chocolate with butter
  2. Mix the sugar and eggs
  3. Add flour and cocoa
  4. Bake in oven (180°C) for 25 minutes
  5. Serve cold with

Mango Kunafa

  1. Mix kunafa dough with melted butter
  2. Bake until golden
  3. Sweeten with syrup while warm
  4. Layer with cream and mango
  5. Top with more mango and serve chilled

Donuts

  1. Mix ingredients and knead dough
  2. Let it rise
  3. Shape into circles
  4. Fry in hot until golden
  5. Add sugrar or chocolate on top

Pancakes

  1. Mix all ingredients until smooth batter
  2. pour small amounts in a hot pan
  3. Cook until bubbles appear, then flip
  4. Serve with honey, syrup or strawberry
