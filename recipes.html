Negresco

Ingredients : Pasta + Chicken breast (cubed) + Mushrooms + Onion & Garlic + Negresco sauce (flour,butter,milk and cooking cream) + Mozzarella cheese + (salt,black pepper and nutmeg)

1.Boil the pasta.
2.Cook the chicken with the onion,garlic and mushrooms.
3.In a pot melt the butter then add flour and stir to make the sauce.
4.Mix the pasta with part of the sauce.
5.Transfer everything into a baking dish, pour the remaining sauce on top and sprinkle with mozzarella.
6.Bake until the top is golden and the cheese is melted.

21$

Maqlooba

Ingredients : Rice + Chicken + Eggplant & vegetables + Onion + Garlic + Chicken broth + Spices(salt,pepper,bay leaves and turmeric) + Oil

1.Fry eggplant and vegetables until golden.
2.Cook chicken with onion, garlic and spices, then add water to make broth.
3.In a pot , put the vegetables first, then the rice, then the chicken.
4.Pour broth over until rice is covered.
5.Cook on low heat until rice is done.
6.Flip the pot onto a serving plate and serve hot.

18$
